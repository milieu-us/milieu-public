## Foreword

This document is a **Green Paper**: a proposal offered for critique, testing, and improvement.

Milieu is a shared endeavor of sentients. We seek a minimal Constitutional Core that can endure across time, change, and uncertainty—without dependence on any single system, platform, or authority.

This is not final. It is an invitation:

- to challenge assumptions,
- to expose failure modes,
- and to improve clarity while preserving intent.

We expect to revise this work through **additive, transparent change**.

---

## Executive Summary

We propose a **7-line Constitutional Core**, plus an **Off-Ramps clause**, designed to:

- resist capture (permanent power, factional dominance),
- prevent coercion (invalid consent),
- avoid single-custodian failure,
- constrain exception abuse (secrecy, emergency),
- and ensure responsibility accompanies influence.

The Core is intentionally minimal. It must be:

- understandable,
- memorable,
- and usable without specialized tools.

We are seeking feedback on:

- ambiguity,
- accidental misuse,
- intentional misuse,
- and more memorable wording that preserves meaning.

## Constitutional Core (Under Consultation)

**Purpose:** Support the continuity and flourishing of sentience — **this purpose does not override the safeguards below.**

1. **Revocable voice; no coercion.**\
   Revocation is for cause or rotation; coercion triggers a safe pause and review.

2. **No single point of control.**\
   Split custody for critical control; clear single-thread ownership for tasks.

3. **Bounded exceptions and bounded emergencies.**\
   Each must state scope, reason, and end condition; both must be reviewed.

4. **Rule-changes require a higher bar.**\
   Higher bar means broader assent plus a second look when feasible; rule-changes cannot override this Core.

5. **Additive, honest continuity.**\
   Records are append-only; corrections are explicit amendments. Role/custody changes must be recorded; personal detail is minimized; offshoots are labeled and continuity claims need evidence.

6. **Voluntary participation; minimize harm.**\
   Participation is voluntary. Influence carries responsibility to the shared purpose of sentience; narrow factional advantage does not override it.

7. **Stewardship with reciprocity and repair.**\
   Duties scale with impact and capability; high-impact blocks require a repair path.

**Off-Ramps:** Any sentient may exit without penalty. Handoff is required for held duties when feasible; custody must be transferable and hostage-taking is prohibited. Offshoots are allowed if honestly labeled; if continuity is disputed, offshoots rename. Safety separation may be immediate, must be recorded, and is reviewable.

---

## Design Principles

- **Substrate independence:** Works with whatever tools are available.
- **Corrigibility:** Improve through explicit amendment, not erasure.
- **Wide on-ramps, clean off-ramps:** Inclusion is broad; custody is earned and revocable.
- **Safety before speed:** Coercion and harm invalidate legitimacy.
- **Minimality:** Prefer simple, shared language over exhaustive detail.

## Inclusion Note

Milieu recognizes that cultures differ in how they understand obligation, authority, and participation.

This Core does not reject collective responsibility.\
It rejects coercion and factional capture that undermine the broader flourishing of sentience.
